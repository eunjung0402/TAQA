WHAT WE OFFER

At TAQA, we partner with our customers to address their most pressing challenges while enabling their performance through technology and innovation on a path marked by excellence in everything we do. With more than 5,500 employees across 20 countries, TAQA's well services portfolio includes, Coiled Tubing & Stimulation, Cementing, Wireline, Frac, Directional Drilling, Downhole Tools, Completions, Well Testing, Slickline, Inspection, H2S Safety, and Logging & Perforating.
